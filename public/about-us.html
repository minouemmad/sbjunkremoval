Our Story

SB Junk Removal was founded with a simple mission: to provide fast, friendly, and reliable junk removal services to our community. We are a small family-owned business that has grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to customer satisfaction and eco-friendly practices.

Our journey began with a single truck and a passion for helping people declutter their lives. Over the years, we've expanded our fleet and our team, but our core values have remained the same. We believe in treating our customers like family, offering personalized services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

At SB Junk Removal, we handle everything from residential cleanouts to commercial projects. Whether you're clearing out your garage, renovating your office, or managing a construction site, we have the expertise and equipment to get the job done efficiently and responsibly. Our team is trained to handle a variety of materials, including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, and construction debris.

Environmental responsibility is at the heart of our operations. We are committed to reducing waste and promoting recycling. Whenever possible, we donate usable items to local charities and ensure that recyclable materials are processed properly. Our goal is to minimize the amount of junk that ends up in landfills and contribute positively to our community and the planet.

We take pride in our prompt and professional service. Our team arrives on time, works quickly, and leaves your space clean and clutter-free. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we go above and beyond to exceed expectations. We are licensed, insured, and dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality.

Thank you for considering SB Junk Removal for your junk removal needs. We look forward to serving you and demonstrating why we are the preferred choice for junk removal in our community. Contact us today to schedule a free, no-obligation quote and experience the SB Junk Removal difference for yourself!

Our Values

At SB Junk Removal, we believe in the power of community, the importance of sustainability, and the value of hard work. These principles guide everything we do, from how we treat our customers to how we handle the items we collect.

Our Mission

To provide top-notch junk removal services while promoting environmental sustainability and community well-being.
