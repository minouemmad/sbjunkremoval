Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of junk removal services including residential cleanouts, commercial cleanouts, estate cleanouts, and more. Our team is equipped to handle jobs of all sizes.

How do I schedule a service?

You can schedule a service by calling us at 319-318-2303, emailing us at iowajunkremoval@gmail.com, or using our online booking system on our website. We offer flexible scheduling options to fit your needs.

What items do you remove?

We remove a variety of items including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and more. If you have specific items in question, please contact us for clarification.

Are you eco-friendly?

Yes, we are committed to eco-friendly disposal methods. We recycle and donate items whenever possible to minimize waste and support our community.

What are your rates?

Our rates are competitive and depend on the volume and type of items being removed. We provide free, no-obligation quotes. The best quotes are done in person to give you the most accurate estimate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. Our team is trained to handle your items with care and professionalism.

How soon can you come for a pickup?

We strive to accommodate your schedule as much as possible. Typically, we can schedule a pickup within 12 to 24 of your request, depending on availability.

Do I need to be present for the pickup?

It's preferred but not mandatory. If you won't be present, please ensure that access to the items is arranged and any special instructions are communicated to our team.

Do you offer same-day services?

Yes, in some cases, we can provide same-day services depending on our availability and your location. Please contact us for inquiries regarding same-day service.

Do you provide cleanup after removal?

Yes, our team ensures that the area is thoroughly cleaned after the removal process. We leave your space tidy and free of debris.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Johnson County Iowa and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm if we operate in your area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments. Please confirm payment options when scheduling your service.

Do you have size or weight limitations?

While we handle items of all sizes, there may be restrictions on extremely large or heavy items. Contact us with details about your items for clarification.

Can you remove hazardous materials?

Yes, we can handle hazardous materials such as chemicals, asbestos, or biohazardous waste. However, we charge extra.

Is there anything I need to do before the pickup?

Ensure that the items to be removed are accessible and clear of obstacles. If there are any specific instructions, please inform us in advance.

Can you provide a certificate of disposal?

Upon request, we can provide a certificate of disposal for your records, confirming that your items have been responsibly disposed of.

Do you offer discounts for recurring services?

Yes, we offer discounts for recurring services. Contact us to discuss a customized plan tailored to your needs.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.

Can you provide references or testimonials?

Yes, we can provide references or testimonials from satisfied customers upon request. Feel free to ask us for more information.
