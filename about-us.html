About SB Junk Removal

Family-owned junk removal service serving Eastern Iowa with environmentally responsible solutions since 2024

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Ben Barker, SB Junk Removal began as a small local business with one truck and a commitment to providing honest, reliable junk removal services to our Iowa City neighbors. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of dedicated professionals serving all of Eastern Iowa, but we've never lost sight of our roots.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted junk removal services in the region, known for our eco-friendly approach, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. Our growth has been fueled by word-of-mouth recommendations from satisfied customers who appreciate our no-nonsense approach to junk removal.

Our Mission

To provide fast, affordable, and environmentally responsible junk removal services while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and community involvement.
